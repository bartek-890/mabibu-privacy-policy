Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MabibuStudio ("we", "us", or "our") manages information through our Pinterest API integration (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a secure experience while using our automation tools.

1. Information We Access

Our App interacts with the Pinterest API to facilitate the scheduling and creation of Pins. Through the official OAuth process, we may access:

  • Profile Data: Your Pinterest username and profile link.
  • Content Data: Titles, descriptions, and images of Pins created through our tool.
  • Authentication Tokens: Secure access tokens provided by Pinterest to perform actions on your behalf.

2. How Information is Used

The data retrieved via the Pinterest API is used exclusively to:

  • Create and publish Pins to your designated boards.
  • Schedule future content to maintain your brand's presence.
  • Ensure the technical stability of the API connection.

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Security and Retention

Access tokens are stored securely and encrypted. We do not store your account password. Data is only kept for the duration necessary to fulfill the automated tasks requested by you. You may revoke access at any time through your Pinterest account settings.

4. Compliance

Our App strictly adheres to the Pinterest Developer Terms and Community Guidelines. We do not support or engage in spamming activities.
